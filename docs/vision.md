# Sovereign — Vision

## North Star

Your AI, your hardware, your data. Privacy-first personal AI that you own completely.

## The Problem

Every major AI assistant — Alexa, Siri, Google Assistant — sends your most intimate data to corporate servers. Your conversations, habits, preferences, and daily routines fuel advertising models you never consented to. You traded privacy for convenience and got neither.

## The Solution

Sovereign is an open-source personal AI stack that runs entirely on your own hardware. It learns from YOUR context — your documents, your calendar, your habits — without any data ever leaving your network.

## Principles

1. **Your Data Stays Home** — Zero telemetry, zero cloud dependencies. Everything runs on hardware you physically control.
2. **Transparent by Default** — Every model, every inference, every decision is auditable. No black boxes.
3. **Community-Driven Intelligence** — A marketplace of open-source models contributed by the community, vetted for safety and performance.
4. **Human-First AI** — Technology that amplifies human capability without replacing human agency. You stay in control.
5. **Offline-Capable** — Full functionality without an internet connection. Your AI works when the cloud doesn't.

## Who It's For

- Privacy-conscious professionals who refuse to send sensitive data to third parties
- Developers who want full control over their AI stack
- Families who want smart home AI without corporate surveillance
- Organizations that need on-premise AI with zero data leakage

## Competitive Positioning

| Feature | Alexa/Siri/Google | Sovereign |
|---------|-------------------|-----------|
| Data Location | Corporate cloud | Your hardware |
| Model Choice | Proprietary, locked | Open-source, swappable |
| Offline Mode | Limited/None | Full capability |
| Privacy | Trust-based | Verifiable |
| Customization | Minimal | Complete |
| Cost | Subscription + data | One-time hardware |
